What is MakerHQ?

Our Mission

MakerHQ is a nonprofit organization that exists to provide a collaborative community space focused on harnessing the technical, artistic, and scientific elements of our city through endeavours in education, research, and team projects.

Our Space

Currently, we have a 700 sq. ft facility conveniently located at 16th Street and X in Sacramento's Broadway neighborhood. This neighborhood is home to some of the most diverse food and cultural establishments in the entire city of Sacramento.

At our size, we're ideally looking to provide desktop fabrication services with the following tools:

  • Desktop CnC Router
  • Desktop 3D Printer
  • Hand tools
  • Reflow Oven
  • Soldering Irons
  • Multimeter
  • Osciliscope
  • Desktop Laser Cutter -- Coming Soon!
  • Embedded Systems for Testing
