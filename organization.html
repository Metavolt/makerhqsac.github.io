What is MakerHQ?

Our Mission

MakerHQ is a nonprofit organization that exists to provide a collaborative community space focused on harnessing the technical, artistic, and scientific elements of our city through endeavours in education, research, and team projects.

MakerHQ Organization

Our organization is a California Nonprofit Benefit Corporation with tax exempt status sponsored by the School Factory. School Factory is an incredible organization dedicated to liberating education and supporting collaborative makerspaces, hackerspaces, and workspaces around the country!

We are currently in the process of filing for our own 501(c)3 tax exempt status and hope to achieve this status soon.
