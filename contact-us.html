Getting Here / Parking

MakerHQ is centrally located right off major freeways in Midtown Sacramento at the corner of 16th st and X st.

There is a light rail station a few blocks away at 20th and Broadway.

Parking options include a fair amount of street parking in the area (pay attention to signs for allowed parking length) or you can use the paid parking lot located across the street next to Willie's Burgers and Chase Bank.

Please do not park in the parking lot of the MakerHQ building. This lot is reserved for other tenants in the building.
